## Main insights

1. **Month 1 retention is weak.** Average Month 1 retention is about 20.6%.
2. **Retention drops quickly after the first purchase.** The business should act before the first month ends.
3. **High-value customers are very important.** The top 20% of customers generate about 74.7% of revenue.
4. **The United Kingdom dominates revenue**, but some smaller countries have high CLTV because their customers place larger or more frequent orders.

## Recommendations

- Start a re-engagement email or discount campaign 2–3 weeks after first purchase.
- Build a simple loyalty offer for repeat buyers.
- Track cohort retention every month as a regular business health metric.
- Use CLTV by segment when deciding how much to spend on customer acquisition.
